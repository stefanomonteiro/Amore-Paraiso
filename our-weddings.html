Our Weddings

We celebrate weddings in paradise! And the most important thing for us is to make this a perfect and smooth day for you as a couple. We care about our clients needs and style and we give you the best advice so it can be a smooth and amazing day, that is why we will want to communicate with you, your needs, your doubts, we will always be there for you during the planning process and day of ceremony so,

Let's Meet

Planning a wedding can be immensely stressful and intensely time consuming, especially when it is a destination wedding in Costa Rica! So many things to think about! Foreign language, hotel accomodations, find vendors you can trust...

It can be very challenging...

Thankfully you can trust me!

I will guide you through every step of the wedding planning process covering all creative, organizational, technical and logistical aspects of your wedding celebration. It will be a one stop shop, so you won't have to deal with different vendors and try to put it all together.

From now on you can consider me your best friend in Costa Rica, I will make sure you have a smooth and happy planning.

But first of all we need to meet each other! You have to get to know me so you can trust me and I would love to meet you, get to know your style, discuss your needs and expectations in detail  so we can design your wedding day together. Communication is key to success so, Yes! I want to know the most I can so I can understand your wishes

Let's set up a Skype coffee date and let's start the planning of the wedding of your dreams!

Personalized

We do not do two exact weddings, our mission is to make a perfect day for "you" as a couple not a  standard package day.

Your wedding should be special, filled with details that show your personality. We will include what you like, we will respect your taste and preferences because your wedding is a day to share who you are as a couple with your loved ones.

We respect your desires and budget, we will find a perfect fit for the things you wish;  finding the right venue, seeking and managing the caterer according with your food preferences, finding musical entertainment that you love and creating the decorations according to your style and personality.

You can keep the wedding as  traditional, but we encourage you to incorporate your style to this wedding! Put your personal signature to it!

Don’t wait any longer!

Trust us with your wedding plans We’d love to hear your ideas, your dreams and make your marriage the best day of your life

Planning

After our initial meeting you will choose your own personalized package, from our services portfolio.

You do not have to choose them all, just choose the ones according to your needs. After this we will send you a proposal with the services you would like to hire, we signed the contract and we a ready to start planning!

Firstly, we will help you find your perfect wedding venue (beach, indoors, mountain, gorgeous beach house…). Secondly, together we select your caterer, floral designer, DJ, wedding cake designer, photographer, stylist … We will pull it all together!

Throughout the wedding planning process we provide advice and guidance via email, phone and Skype because communication is essential for a smooth planning.
