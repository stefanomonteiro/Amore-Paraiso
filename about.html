Who We Are

My name is Erika Barreiro, I'm the founder of Amore Paraiso and your Wedding Planner in Costa Rica! I fell in love with this inspiring Country, It's lifestyle, beaches, mountains, waterfalls and views, living in Costa Rica encouraged me to find my passion and to create a life I adore. I am a lawyer who decided to follow my heart and do what I love the most; helping you to plan, design  and create a beautiful wedding, surrounded with nature, good vibes and pura vida, so I started my own wedding planner services in Costa Rica. Together we can create your dream, authentic wedding in Costa Rica.  You don't have to worry any longer for consulting all the vendors by yourself, trying to communicate and trying to make everything fit. I provide expert advise for any aspect of your wedding planning in Costa Rica: such as dealing with paperwork, sourcing local trustable suppliers (venues, photographers, flowers…),setting up and planning wonderful tours and adventures for you and your guests and also giving you creative ideas with a tropical twist! Getting married in Costa Rica is not complicated, but it could be a hassle if you do not know the language or you are not familiar with the Costa Rican culture. For these reasons I  can help you in many ways. I speak your language and the local language, I know the best vendors in town and I can make your wedding planning easy and fun. Do not hesitate to contact me and tell me about your wedding idea –  I will give you initial suggestions which will reflect your style as well as your budget.
