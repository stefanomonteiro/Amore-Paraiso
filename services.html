Our Services

Planning a wedding at a different country can be very daunting! That is why we are here for you, our bilingual team pull it all together so you dont have to crack your head finding the right venue and the best vendors, we design your wedding according to your style and make sure everything runs smooth and easy! This way all you have to worry about is having fun!

You can choose all of our services or make a specific list of services according to your needs!
