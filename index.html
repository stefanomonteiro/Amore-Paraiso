Congratulations You are engaged!

This is such an exciting moment! breath, relax and take your time to enjoy it. You’ve got lots of other big decisions ahead of you to make your wedding a day to remember, and we are right here to help you and make your Costa Rica wedding as smooth and fun as possible!

If you are here is because you have been thinking about an amazing destination wedding in Costa Rica. And let us tell you:  You are on the right path to an unforgettable celebration!

Costa Rica is a gorgeous country, with beautiful nature and outstanding beaches and volcanoes. There is so much to do and see! You can choose from many types of mouth-dropping venues where you can have your ceremony: right on the beach, at a lake or with volcanoes and mountains views.

Besides, you will be able to not only share with your loved ones your wedding day and have a fun celebration, but you also get the opportunity to enjoy many adventures with your guests before the wedding! Hot-springs, volcano viewing, catamaran sailing you name it!

  Costa Rica is filled with adventures!

We are here to make everything possible and easier for you.

Think of us as your one-stop shop in Costa Rica for your wedding, we take care of every detail assisting you and creating a memorable, magical celebration. Our personal approach ensures the weddings we plan are meaningful and truly reflect our clients as individuals, as couples, and states something about their shared values and sense of style.

Let's start planning!
